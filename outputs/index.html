Problem background

Youth unemployment is not only a job vacancy problem.

Many young job seekers struggle because training information, vacancy details, interview preparation, and career guidance are scattered across different platforms. The previous project correctly identified unemployment and skills mismatch, but the website mostly presented information instead of guiding users through action.

Skills mismatch

Graduates may not have the communication, digital, workplace, or technical skills requested by employers.

Low awareness

Training programmes exist, but users may not know which are suitable, current, or credible.

Search barriers

Job seekers often waste time moving between job boards without clear requirements or next steps.

Weak support

Resume, interview, and follow-up guidance is often missing after users find an opportunity.

Proposed solution

A guided career pathway, not just a list of links.

TRAIN TO WORK + turns the previous project into a more interactive and user-friendly career readiness system.

01
